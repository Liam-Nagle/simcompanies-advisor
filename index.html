Session Setup

This app needs your SimCompanies session cookie to fetch your data via the proxy.
This is a one-time step — it stays saved until your session expires.

1Open simcompanies.com in this browser and make sure you're logged in.
2Press F12 to open DevTools, then click the Network tab.
3Reload the SimCompanies page (Ctrl+R).
4Click any request to www.simcompanies.com in the list.
5Scroll down in the right panel to Request Headers and find the cookie: line. Click it and copy the entire value.
